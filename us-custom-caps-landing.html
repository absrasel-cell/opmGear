FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

CUSTOMIZATION ANSWERS

Everything you need to know about our custom cap and patch services, from design to delivery.

What patch types do you offer?

We specialize in embroidery, rubber, printed, and woven patches with premium materials and thread options.

How does wholesale pricing work?

Wholesale orders start at 50 units with tiered discounts: 10% for 100+, 20% for 500+ units.

What are minimum orders?

Individual orders start at 1 unit. Wholesale pricing begins at 50 units with volume discounts.

What's your production timeline and delivery schedule?

Standard orders: 7-10 business days. Rush orders available in 3-5 days. Wholesale orders typically 10-15 business days depending on quantity and complexity.

What materials & caps do you use?

Premium cotton, polyester, and mesh caps with professional-grade threads and materials for all patch types.

Do you offer design services?

Yes! Our professional design team can create custom artwork or optimize your existing designs for production.
